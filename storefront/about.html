History

Just Burgers and Fries was founded on the belief that hamburgers and fries are two very beautiful things, especially together. Everything else on the menu of a fast food joint is really just a waste of space. Along with streamlining the menu, our fearless creators streamlined more costs by eliminating any kind of cashier service withn an online only ordering system. Both of these simplifications allow for the amazingly low prices our fans have come to know and love! Just Burgers and Fries has been faithfully serving members of the Salt Lake City community since July of 2024!
