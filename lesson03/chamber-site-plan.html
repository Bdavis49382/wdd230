Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Urithiru Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Welcome to the Urithiru Chamber of Commerce website! Explore local businesses, events, and attractions. Whether you’re a resident or a visitor, we’re here to connect you with the heart of Urithiru’s vibrant community.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the chamber of commerce?
  • What events are happening that I can participate in?
  • How can I contact someone to get more information from a real person?

Color Palette

  • light background (the general background for the page)
  • dark background (the background in highlighted areas such as the header and footer)
  • accent background (the background for accented elements such as buttons)
  • accent text (text color for important text that should stand out)

Typography

  • Title Font (Nunito Sans - Bold)
  • General Font (Montserrat)
