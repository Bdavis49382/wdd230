Membership Options

NP Membership(Free)

  • Listing in Chamber Directory: Get your organization's name and contact details listed in our official chamber directory.
  • Access to Networking Events: Attend networking sessions, workshops, and seminars to connect with other local businesses.
  • Monthly Newsletter: Stay informed about community news, events, and opportunities.
  • Community Support: Be part of a network that supports local causes and initiatives.

Fee: $0

Bronze Membership

  • All Non-Profit Benefits: Enjoy the benefits of the Non-Profit membership.
  • Enhanced Directory Listing: Highlight your business with a logo and brief description.
  • Exclusive Workshops: Participate in specialized workshops tailored for Bronze members.
  • Discounted Event Tickets: Access chamber events at discounted rates.
  • Social Media Shout-Outs: Get featured on our social media platforms.

Fee: $50 per year

Silver Membership

  • All Bronze Benefits: Access all the perks of the Bronze membership.
  • Business Spotlight: Be featured in our monthly newsletter or website.
  • Priority Referrals: Receive priority referrals from the chamber.
  • Business Advocacy: Participate in advocacy efforts for local businesses.
  • Chamber Logo Usage: Display the chamber logo on your website and marketing materials.

Fee: $150 per year

Gold Membership

  • All Silver Benefits: Enjoy all the advantages of the Silver membership.
  • VIP Networking: Attend exclusive VIP networking events.
  • Leadership Opportunities: Serve on chamber committees or task forces.
  • Prominent Directory Placement: Top placement in the chamber directory.
  • Sponsorship Opportunities: Sponsor chamber events and gain visibility.

Fee: $300 per year
